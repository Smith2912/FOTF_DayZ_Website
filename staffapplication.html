Staff Application

We're always looking for dedicated members to join our staff team. As a staff member, you'll help maintain our friendly environment and assist players on our DayZ server.

Staff Roles

Junior Moderator

Monitor chat, assist players with basic questions, and report rule violations.

Moderator

Handle player disputes, enforce server rules, and assist with community events.

Admin

Manage server operations, oversee moderators, and help develop community policies.

Requirements

  • At least 18 years of age
  • Regular activity on our server
  • Friendly and helpful attitude
  • Good communication skills
  • Experience with DayZ (preferred but not required)
  • Discord presence and participation

How to Apply

All staff applications are processed through our Discord server. Join our Discord using the button below and look for the staff-application channel to submit your application.
